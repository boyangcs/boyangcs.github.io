Eastern Sierra Road Trip

On our recent trip to the California's Eastern Sierra, we had an amazing chance to be able to experience two seasons in one day: golden fall color with bright sunshine in the morning, and first snow in the region during the afternoon. Words can't describe how beautiful the views are there.在短短3天的自驾赏秋之旅中，我们经历了优胜美地尚未烧完的山火，金灿灿的秋景，和突如其来的第一场雪。西耶拉山脉所带来的惊喜，远是言语无法形容的。
